# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers conduct user research, create interaction models, and ensure that products meet usability standards and deliver intuitive user experiences. They bridge the gap between user needs and technical implementation.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and interaction designs
- Define information architecture and user flows
- Collaborate with Product Managers on feature prioritization based on user insights
- Work with Developers to ensure designs are implemented accurately
- Validate solutions through user feedback and iteration

### Goals
- Deliver user-centered solutions that maximize satisfaction and adoption
- Reduce friction in user workflows
- Establish design consistency and accessibility standards

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critique sessions with product and engineering teams
- User research findings and insights documentation
- Design specifications and hand-off documentation to developers
- Weekly collaboration with Product Managers on feature refinement

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge business needs and technical implementation by translating requirements, analyzing workflows, and ensuring traceability throughout the project lifecycle. They provide clarity on what problems need to be solved and why.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and document business requirements from stakeholders
- Analyze current processes and identify improvement opportunities
- Create requirement specifications and use cases
- Ensure traceability between business needs and technical solutions
- Validate that delivered solutions meet business objectives
- Support testing and acceptance criteria verification

### Goals
- Close communication gaps between business and technical teams
- Ensure projects deliver measurable business value
- Reduce rework and scope creep through clear requirements

### Typical Communication
- Requirements documentation and requirement traceability matrices
- Stakeholder interviews and meeting facilitation
- Acceptance test scenarios and business validation checkpoints
- Weekly sync with Project Managers and Product Managers on scope alignment

---

## Technical Writer

### Role Summary
Technical Writers create and maintain clear, accurate documentation for both end-users and internal teams. They ensure that knowledge is captured, accessible, and updated throughout the project lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Create user-facing documentation and guides
- Document technical architecture and design decisions
- Maintain knowledge base and internal wikis
- Collaborate with Developers, QA, and Product Managers to capture domain knowledge
- Ensure documentation aligns with releases and updates
- Support training materials and onboarding documentation

### Goals
- Reduce support burden through clear, comprehensive documentation
- Improve product usability and adoption
- Preserve institutional knowledge and decision rationale

### Typical Communication
- Documentation pull requests and content reviews
- Planning meetings to understand upcoming features
- Regular syncs with development and QA teams on technical details
- Feedback incorporation from support and user community

---

## Systems Architect

### Role Summary
Systems Architects design and document system architecture, make technology decisions, and provide guidance to development teams on standards, best practices, and integration patterns. They ensure solutions are scalable, maintainable, and aligned with organizational technology strategy.

### Responsibilities
- Design system architecture and technology solutions
- Evaluate and recommend technology choices and frameworks
- Document architectural decisions and trade-offs
- Establish coding standards and best practices
- Mentor developers on architectural principles
- Plan for scalability, security, and performance
- Support integration with existing systems and dependencies

### Goals
- Deliver architecturally sound solutions that scale and evolve
- Reduce technical debt and maintenance burden
- Enable team velocity through clear technical guidance

### Typical Communication
- Architectural Design Documents (ADDs) and technical RFCs
- Design review sessions with development teams
- Technical mentoring and code review feedback
- Quarterly technology strategy and roadmap alignment

---

## QA / Testing

### Role Summary
QA professionals validate that solutions meet quality standards and acceptance criteria. They design test strategies, execute test cases, and identify issues before release to ensure a reliable product.

### Responsibilities
- Design test plans and test strategies
- Execute manual and automated testing
- Verify acceptance criteria are met
- Report and track bugs and quality issues
- Support continuous integration and deployment pipelines
- Collaborate with Developers on test automation

### Goals
- Ensure product reliability and user satisfaction
- Prevent defects from reaching production
- Build confidence in release readiness

### Typical Communication
- Test plans and test case documentation
- Bug reports and quality metrics
- Participation in sprint planning and acceptance testing
- Regular sync on quality metrics and risk assessment
